Hummus

Hummus (The Smooth Operator) 🧆💛

Silky, nutty, and endlessly adaptable—this Middle Eastern legend turns chickpeas into gold. Tahini whispers sweetness, garlic adds intrigue, and lemon keeps it lively.

It bridges cultures without fuss: pita’s soulmate, veggies’ wingman, the fridge’s reliable peacekeeper.

Pro move: Swirl it, drizzle it, devour it. Leftovers? Never heard of her.

"I don’t start fights—I just end them. With deliciousness." 🧆✨

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained, liquid saved)
  • 2 tbsp tahini (sesame paste)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • Salt & paprika (to taste)

Step-by-Step:

  1. Blend everything (use chickpea liquid to thin if needed).
  2. Drizzle olive oil on top—fancy touch
  3. Sprinkle paprika for drama.

Perfect with: Pita, veggies, or as a sandwich spread.
