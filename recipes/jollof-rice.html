Classic Ghana Jollof Rice

This recipe guides you through creating a vibrant and deeply flavourful Jollof Rice, a beloved staple across West Africa. The key is to build a rich, aromatic tomato and pepper base before slowly steaming the rice to perfection.

Serves: 4 – 6
Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 50 – 60 minutes
