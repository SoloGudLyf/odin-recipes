Pico de Gallo: The Fresh-Faced Rebel

No mush, no blenders—just chopped chaos in a bowl. Tomatoes bring the juice, onions add the sass, and jalapeños? Pure danger.

It’s the crunch your tacos crave, the zing your chips deserve.

Warning: Highly addictive. Side effects may include stealing the spotlight from main dishes. 🌶️🍅

"I’m not salsa. I’m what salsa wishes it could be."

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • ¼ cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeño, minced (seeds removed for less heat)
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Optional: 1 small tomato (diced), 1 garlic clove (minced), pinch of cumin

  1. Step 1: Prep Like a Pro

    Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

    Mash lightly with a fork—keep it chunky or go smooth, your call!

  2. Step 2: Flavor Bomb Time

    Add lime juice immediately (keeps it fresh + adds tang).

    Toss in onion, jalapeño, cilantro, and salt. Mix gently.

    Optional: Stir in tomato, garlic, or cumin if you’re feeling fancy.

  3. Step 3: Taste & Adjust

    Dip a chip—needs more salt? Lime? Spice? Fix it now.

  4. Step 4: Serve & Destroy

    Eat immediately (guac waits for no one).

    Best with: tortilla chips, tacos, nachos, or straight off the spoon
